Friends of Education

Founded in 2024, Friends of Education is a grassroots initiative committed to making education accessible for underprivileged students. Inspired by my return to my high school after 10 years, we began by sponsoring one student. Today, we've supported nine students—some of whom are orphans—through tuition, books, and uniforms. Our vision is to break cycles of poverty by investing in young minds.

A Played Nation

A Played Nation is my debut book and poetic reflection on political awakening, social betrayal, and the dreams of a new generation. It’s a literary and spoken-word project that speaks to both personal struggle and collective hope. The work highlights the cost of silence and the power of civic participation.
